About this game

This memory game is based on Pokemon! Match pokemon to collect them and watch your pokedex progress on the right. I originally went with the Route 47 music for the background music, but it ended up being a little too stirring for a memory match game, so I went with the rather relaxing music from Azalea Town (the OG version from Gen 2!). The rest of the sound effects are from the Gen 3 and 4 games.
